Top Philippines Destinations

These dream destinations are probably already on your bucket list—and for good reason!

The Travelers’ Choice Awards Best of the Best honors the pinnacle of travel excellence, based on outstanding reviews and ratings from the Tripadvisor community over the past year. Out of 8 million listings, fewer than 1% reach this prestigious milestone.

01.

Boracay

Known for its powdery white sand beaches, Boracay is a tropical paradise famous for vibrant nightlife, water activities, and luxury resorts.

Great for

02.

Palawan (El Nido)

El Nido is a stunning archipelago with dramatic limestone cliffs, hidden lagoons, and some of the clearest waters perfect for diving and island hopping.

Great for

03.

Cebu

A central hub for history, culture, and pristine beaches. Highlights include the historic Magellan’s Cross, Kawasan Falls, and beautiful island getaways like Bantayan and Malapascua.

Great for

04.

Bohol

Home to the iconic Chocolate Hills and tarsier sanctuaries. Bohol also offers stunning beaches, historic churches, and the Loboc River cruise.

Great for

05.

Siargao

Known as the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines," Siargao boasts world-class waves and beautiful lagoons, with a laid-back island atmosphere.

Great for

06.

Coron

A diver's paradise, Coron offers shipwreck diving, crystal-clear lakes like Kayangan Lake, and impressive coral reefs.

Great for

07.

Tagaytay

Just a short drive from Manila, Tagaytay offers cool weather, panoramic views of Taal Volcano, and a peaceful retreat from the city.

Great for

08.

Vigan

A UNESCO World Heritage site, Vigan is known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture and cobblestone streets in the historic Calle Crisologo.

Great for

09.

Batanes

With its rolling hills, dramatic cliffs, and stone houses, Batanes offers breathtaking landscapes and a quiet escape from the busy city life.

Great for

10.

Davao

Famous for Mount Apo, the highest peak in the Philippines, Davao also offers nature reserves, cultural sites, and bustling markets.

Great for

11.

Panglao Island

Located near Bohol, Panglao offers stunning beaches, excellent diving spots, and luxurious resorts perfect for a peaceful beach getaway.

Great for

12.

Batangas

Known for its dive spots like Anilao and the picturesque Taal Lake, Batangas is a popular destination for both adventurers and weekend travelers.

Great for

13.

Zambales

Zambales is known for its beaches and coves, particularly Anawangin Cove and Nagsasa Cove, where visitors can camp and enjoy the pristine coastline.

Great for

14.

Sagada

Famous for its hanging coffins, Sagada is a cool mountain town that offers hiking, spelunking, and the ethereal sunrise at Kiltepan Peak.

Great for

15.

Camiguin

Often referred to as the "Island Born of Fire" due to its many volcanoes, Camiguin is also known for its hot and cold springs, waterfalls, and white-sand islets.

Great for

16.

Subic Bay

A former U.S. naval base turned into a tourist destination, Subic offers adventure parks, diving sites, and duty-free shopping.

Great for

17.

Manila

The bustling capital of the Philippines, Manila is a mix of old and new with historic sites like Intramuros, modern shopping malls, and a vibrant nightlife.

Great for

18.

La Union

A top surfing destination in the north, La Union offers consistent waves, hip beach resorts, and a chill vibe perfect for beach lovers and surfers.

Great for

19.

Puerto Galera

Located on Mindoro Island, Puerto Galera is known for its beaches, coral reefs, and dive spots. It’s also a popular destination for weekend getaways from Manila.

Tuguegarao
